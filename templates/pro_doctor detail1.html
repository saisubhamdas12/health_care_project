About Dr Sandeep Vaishya

Dr Sandeep Vaishya is one of the top Neurosurgeons in India. He has 27+ years of experience in the field & has been associated with some of the best hospitals in India. His specializations are Intracranial Brain Tumour Surgery, Deep Brain Stimulation Surgery, Skull Base Surgery, Brain Surgery, General Surgery, Minimally Invasive Brain, Spine Surgery & Image-Guided Neurosurgery, Peripheral Nerve Surgery, and Brachial Plexus Surgery. Dr. Vaishya has completed his MBBS & MS from the well-known G R Medical College, Gwalior. Following which he did his MCh at the prestigious AIIMS, Delhi. He is an awardee of the Sundt Fellowship at Mayo Clinic, USA. He is one of the leading surgeons in the world for brachial plexus Injuries and in South Asia for Gamma Knife Surgery. He did several successful surgeries in the field of neurosurgery. Dr. Vaishya's treatment for Hydrocephalus was also featured in the popular Netflix documentary Rooting for Roona.
